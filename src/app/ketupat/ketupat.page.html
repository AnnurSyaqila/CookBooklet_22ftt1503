Ketupat Ketupat Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 4 pandan leaves
How to make it:
  • Wash the rice and drain. Put the rice, water, and salt in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  • Lower the heat and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the water has been absorbed.
  • Remove from the heat and let stand for 5 minutes.
  • Cut 4 pieces of 12-inch aluminum foil. Fold each piece in half to form a triangle.
  • Fold the longest side of the triangle in half to form a smaller triangle. Open the foil and place 1/2 cup of rice in the center.
  • Fold the foil over the rice to form a triangle. Fold the edges of the foil over the triangle to seal.
  • Repeat with the remaining rice.
  • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  • Add the rice packets and cook for 2 hours, adding more water as necessary to keep the packets submerged.
  • Remove the packets from the water and drain. Let cool.
  • Remove the rice from the packets and cut into 1/2-inch slices.
  • Serve with coconut milk.
