Roast Beef & Red Wine Sauce Roast Beef & Red Wine Sauce Ingredients:
  • 1 (3-pound) beef tenderloin roast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup minced shallots
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
How to make it:
  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  • Rub beef tenderloin with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  • Place on a rack in a shallow roasting pan.
  • Roast in preheated oven until a meat thermometer inserted into thickest part of meat registers 135 degrees F (57 degrees C) for medium-rare, about 30 minutes.
  • Remove from oven, and let stand 10 minutes before slicing.
  • While beef is roasting, cook shallots in a large skillet over medium heat until softened.
  • Stir in wine and beef broth; bring to a boil, and cook until reduced to about 1/2 cup.
  • Remove from heat, and whisk in butter. Serve with beef.
