How to make it:

  • Add molasses and vanilla and continue to mix until well blended.

 Roast Beef with Red Wine Sauce ingredients:
  • 1 (3-pound) beef tenderloin roast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup minced shallots
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
How to make it:
  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Rub beef tenderloin with olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Place on a rack in a shallow roasting pan.
  • Roast in preheated oven until a meat thermometer inserted into thickest part of meat registers 135 degrees F (57 degrees C) for medium-rare, about 30 minutes. Remove from oven, and let stand 10 minutes before slicing.
  • While beef is roasting, cook shallots in a large skillet over medium heat until softened. Stir in wine and beef broth; bring to a boil, and cook until reduced to about 1/2 cup. Remove from heat, and whisk in butter. Serve with beef.
Halloween Eidul Fitri Ketupat Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 4 pandan leaves
How to make it:
  • Wash the rice and drain. Put the rice, water, and salt in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the water has been absorbed. Remove from the heat and let stand for 5 minutes.
  • Cut 4 pieces of 12-inch aluminum foil. Fold each piece in half to form a triangle. Fold the longest side of the triangle in half to form a smaller triangle. Open the foil and place 1/2 cup of rice in the center. Fold the foil over the rice to form a triangle. Fold the edges of the foil over the triangle to seal. Repeat with the remaining rice.
  • Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the rice packets and cook for 2 hours, adding more water as necessary to keep the packets submerged. Remove the packets from the water and drain. Let cool. Remove the rice from the packets and cut into 1/2-inch slices. Serve with coconut milk.
