Wajid Wajid Ingredients:
  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
How to make it:
  • In a saucepan, combine the rice, water, and coconut milk.
  • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  • In a small bowl, stir together the sugar, salt, and vanilla. Mix the sugar mixture into the rice.
  • Cover and cook for 5 minutes longer.
  • Remove from heat and let stand for a few minutes.
  • Serve warm.
